Guo-Hua Zhang, Professor, is currently working at the State Key Laboratory of Advanced Metallurgy, University of Science and Technology Beijing, China. The main research fields are: i) Physical chemistry of metallurgical melts; ii) Comprehensive utilization of solid waste, such as high titanium bearing slags, stainless steel pickling sludge; iii) Metallurgy of refractory metals, including the preparations of metal powders(Molybdenum, Tungsten), ferroalloys, as well as refractory metal carbides, nitrides, borides and silicides.

Prof. Zhang obtained his doctor's degree of Metallurgical Engineering at University of Science and Technology Beijing in January, 2012. He was promoted to be associate professor and formal professor in 2014 and 2018, respectively. As a visiting scholar, he worked at University of Tokyo (Cooperation tutor: Prof. Tsukihashi Fumitaka, 2013.6-2014.4), and University of Utah (Cooperation tutor: Prof. Zhigang Zak Fang, 2017.9-2018.3). Until now, he as published more than 130 papers indexed by SCI as the first/corresponding author, and was granted more than 10 China patents.
