Ceramic powders of refractory metal bearing carbides, nitrides, silicides

The main achievements are introduced as follows,

1) Metal boride powders. A universal method for the preparation of metal boride was proposed. A low cost process was proposed to prepare high purity ultrafine calcium boride powder, which was used as reductant to reduce metal oxides to prepare the corresponding borides. The method has the advantages of high product purity, controllable particle size.

2) Refractory metal carbides, and nitrides. The universal method for preparing refractory metal carbides and nitride was also proposed. The ultrafine powders with a high purity, uniform particle size, good dispersibility, could be obtained.

3) Tungsten carbide powder. A low cost route to prepare ultrafine tungsten carbide powders was proposed by directly using tungsten oxide as the raw material. The prepared ultrafine powders have the advantages of uniform particle size (nanoscale), high purity and so on.

4) Others. Refractory metal silicides, etc.
