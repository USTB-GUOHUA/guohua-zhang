Comprehensive utilization of solid waste

1) Comprehensive utilization of titanium-containing slags, such as high-titanium electric furnace slags, titanium-containing blast furnace slags, ilmenite, etc, to prepare titanium carbide, titanium nitride and titanium silicide.

2) Hazardous wastes. Hazardous waste containing heavy metals or other harmful elements is harmful to the environment, such as stainless steel pickling sludge. To achieve value-added utilization and harmless treatment of hazardous wastes, the pyrometallurgical method was applied for the extraction of the valuable metal and vitrification treatment of the remaining slags.
