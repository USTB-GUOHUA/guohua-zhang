Refractory metals bearing ferroalloys

The main achievements are introduced as follows,

1) A low cost process was proposed to prepare ferrovanadium nitride, by directly using vanadium oxide as the raw material, which avoids the utilization of ferrovanadium and greatly shortens the production process.

2) The technology for preparing molybdenum-containing steelmaking additives (molybdenum carbide, high quality ferromolybdenum or steel-making molybdenum powder) is proposed by using molybdenite concentrate as raw materials. Compared with the traditional process, the oxidation roasting of molybdenite concentrate is avoided, which discharges lots of low-concentration SO2.

3) Process optimization and refining of the remaining ferroalloy process.
